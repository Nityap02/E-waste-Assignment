"Welcome to Remine India"


Recycling center

Remine India is a startup recognized by the government of India (DPIIT) and is working for responsible recycling of e-waste and Li battery waste in India. Company Registered at dated 06-DEC-2022 on Ministry of Corporate Affairs(MCA).


When it comes to e-waste and lithium-ion battery recycling, Remine India has demonstrated a strong commitment to environmental sustainability and responsible waste management practices. By taking a proactive approach to these issues, Remine India is helping to protect the environment and build a more sustainable future for all.



Know More

Things to know about



E-Waste



E-waste, which includes discarded electronic devices, is a growing concern around the world. These items can contain hazardous materials such as lead, mercury, and cadmium, which can pose significant health and environmental risks if not disposed of properly. Remine India has recognized the importance of e-waste recycling and has implemented a range of initiatives to reduce its impact on the environment.

Lithium-ion battery waste



Remine India's battery recycling program collects used batteries from its customers and processes them in a way that maximizes the recovery of valuable materials such as lithium, cobalt, and nickel. These materials can then be used to manufacture new batteries, reducing the need for virgin materials and minimizing the environmental impact of battery production.
